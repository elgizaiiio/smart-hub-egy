

# تحسين نص صفحة الإحالة

## التغيير
استبدال الـ Headline الحالي (سطرين فقط) بنص تسويقي أقوى وأكثر تفصيلاً:

**الحالي:**
```
Referral Program
20% commission on every payment — forever
```

**الجديد:**
```
20% Forever — No Limits — No Expiry

Every subscriber you refer =
20% every month or every year — Forever

Share your link. They subscribe. You earn 20% of their payment —
not once, not for a year — forever. No caps. No limits. No expiry.
As long as they stay subscribed, you keep earning.
```
